:::info[Updated 4/16/2026]
Our VCC Repositories are starting to slowing come back online! However, <u>only</u> the Toon version will be available on the VCC at this time.

The repository has not received any commits since the issue occurred, so it is <u>**100% safe to use.**</u>
:::

Hello everyone, BluWizard and Tony_Lewis here.

Poiyomi's accounts, including their Google Account, were compromised on April 13th, 2026, as a result of a non-targeted cyber attack on their end. This means packages were temporarily inaccessible for some time until the situation was fully resolved.

## What Happened?

- **Google Account**
  - Their Gmail account was compromised and have been working with Google to secure it.
- **Discord**
  - Poiyomi's Discord Account was partially compromised and Poiyomi still had access, but the associated email account has been compromised.
- **Poiyomi Pro VCC & Pro Website**
  - The Poiyomi Pro VCC and the website, `pro.poiyomi.com`, is currently down and packages hosted on the website have been taken offline early as a precaution.
- **VCC & GitHub**
  - The VCC / GitHub Repositories are back online and are safe to use.
  - **No files have been modified**, and no new commits have been made.
  - **Your existing installation of Poiyomi Shaders are unaffected and SAFE**, and you may continue using them.
- **Twitter/X**
  - Twitter/X account has not been compromised and Poiyomi still has access, but the associated email account was compromised.
- **Poiyomi Docs (this website)**
  - As the source code was attached to Poiyomi's GitHub, it is currently unaccessible. However, I have restored the most recent backup copy of the website and am hosting it on my fork for the time being. (BluWizard)

We appreciate your understanding and support during this time. If you have any concerns or questions about the situation, please feel free to reach out. We will keep everyone updated on the resolution regarding the Google Account.

This Blog Post will be updated when we learn more information.

Thank you for your patience!
