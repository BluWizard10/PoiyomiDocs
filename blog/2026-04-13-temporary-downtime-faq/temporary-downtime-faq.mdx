Hello everyone, BluWizard and Tony_Lewis here. We've got some unfortunate news to share...

Poiyomi's accounts, including their Google Account, have been compromised as a result of a non-targeted cyber attack on their end. This means packages will be inaccessible for the time being until the situation is fully resolved.

## What we know so far

- **Google Account**
  - Their Gmail account was hacked. We are working on getting in touch with Google support to secure it.
- **Discord**
  - Poiyomi's Discord Account was partially compromised and Poiyomi still had access, but the associated email account has been compromised.
- **Poiyomi Pro VCC & Pro Website**
  - The Poiyomi Pro VCC and the website, `pro.poiyomi.com`, is currently down and packages hosted on the website have been taken offline early as a precaution.
- **VCC & GitHub**
  - The VCC / GitHub Repositories are currently offline as a security measure during recovery efforts.
  - **No files have been modified**, and no new commits have been made.
  - **Your existing installation of Poiyomi Shaders are unaffected and SAFE**, and you may continue using them.
- **Twitter/X**
  - Twitter/X account has not been compromised and Poiyomi still has access, but the associated email account has been compromised.
- **Poiyomi Docs (this website)**
  - As the source code was attached to Poiyomi's GitHub, it is currently unaccessible. However, I have restored the most recent backup copy of the website and am hosting it on my end for the time being. (BluWizard)

We appreciate your understanding and support during this time. If you have any concerns or questions about the situation, please feel free to reach out. We will keep everyone updated on the resolution regarding the Google Account.

This Blog Post will be updated when we learn more information.

Thank you for your patience!

## FAQ

<details>
<summary><b>I have Poiyomi Shaders installed in my Project. Is it safe?</b></summary>

**Yes.** Since our packages can be worked on offline, they do not need an internet connection. You are safe to continue using it.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Have the packages been compromised?</b></summary>

**No.** There have been <u>no</u> changes made to the packages. They are still safe to use.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What happened to the VCC Repositories?</b></summary>

They have been **voluntarily** taken offline as a security precaution.

There have been <u>no</u> changes to the files in the VCC versions whatsoever prior to their downtime. They are just offline as a precaution.

We understand this can be frustrating to you and are working as fast as possible to restore the VCC Repositories. We greatly appreciate your patience at this time.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How can I download a copy of Poiyomi Toon?</b></summary>

Poiyomi Toon is currently unavailable as our GitHub Repositories are down. They are only available in our Discord for the time being.

We are working as fast as possible to restore access to download a copy of Poiyomi Toon from our verified sources outside of Discord. We appreciate your patience at this time.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>I just recently subscribed to the Patreon. Can I download Poiyomi Pro?</b></summary>

Poiyomi Pro's latest version is available in our Discord Server temporarily until our main website comes back online.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>If I'm a current Patreon subscriber, is my personal information at risk?</b></summary>

No. Our Patreon <u>DOES NOT</u> show sensitive personal information from backers. **You are safe.**

If you want to be extra vigilant though, review your account's security settings if you want to be extra safe.

</details>
